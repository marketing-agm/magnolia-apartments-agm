About the home

The kind of find people tell their friends about.

“Upper-floor homes capture breathtaking views of the Magnolia neighborhood and the Seattle skyline beyond.”

Six homes · Studio – 2 BR · Magnolia, Seattle

Magnolia Crestview is a small, well-kept building with a handful of homes — studios through generously sized two-bedrooms. The two-bedroom plans are cavernous by Seattle standards: full updated kitchens, two bathrooms in the largest layouts, abundant closet space, and private decks with built-in storage.

Every layout was drawn to maximize daily livability. Real bedrooms with real closets. Pass-through kitchens, not galleys. Outdoor space that's actually yours. It's a building that works whether you're solo, partnered, or sharing the rent.

Homes don't come available often, and when they do, they go fast. Six are open now.
