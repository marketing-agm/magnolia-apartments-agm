Nine homes · 1 BR – 2 BR · Magnolia, Seattle

Magnolia Crestview is a small, well-kept building with a handful of homes — bright one-bedrooms and generously sized two-bedrooms. The two-bedroom plans are spacious by Seattle standards: full updated kitchens, two bathrooms, abundant closet space, and private decks with built-in storage.

Every layout was drawn to maximize daily livability. Real bedrooms with real closets. Pass-through kitchens, not galleys. Outdoor space that's actually yours. It's a building that works whether you're solo, partnered, or sharing the rent.

    Questions, answered

    Frequently asked

    Renting in Magnolia, in plain terms. Still curious? Call (206) 694-1714 or schedule a tour.

    Where is Magnolia Crestview located?

    Magnolia Crestview is at 2701 W Manor Pl, Seattle, WA 98199 — in the heart of Seattle's Magnolia neighborhood, a short walk from Discovery Park and Magnolia Village.

    How much is rent, and what floor plans are available?

    We offer one-bedroom homes (about 600 sq ft) starting at $1,595/month and two-bedroom, two-bath homes (about 1,000 sq ft) up to $1,995/month. See current availability and floor plans for live pricing.

    Is there a move-in special right now?

    Yes — one month free on a 12-month lease for current move-ins. Specials can change, so confirm the latest offer when you schedule a tour.

    Is parking available?

    Yes. The building has both covered and uncovered parking options on-site.

    Is Magnolia Crestview pet-friendly?

    Yes, the community is pet-friendly. Contact the leasing office at (206) 694-1714 for the current pet policy, deposits, and any breed or weight guidelines.

    What are the rental application and screening requirements?

    Applicants are screened for a credit score of 650 or higher with minimal negative reporting, 1–2 years of positive rental history, and a combined household income of about 3× the monthly rent. Seattle applications are processed in accordance with the First-in-Time Ordinance (SMC 14.08).

    How close is Discovery Park, and what else is nearby?

    Discovery Park — 534 acres of trails, beaches, and a historic lighthouse — is just minutes away. You're also near Magnolia Village's shops, cafés, and weekend farmers market, with downtown Seattle a short drive away. Explore the neighborhood map for distances and travel times.

    How do I schedule a tour or apply?

    Use the tour scheduler on this page to pick a time, or call (206) 694-1714. Our leasing team will walk you through the application from there.
