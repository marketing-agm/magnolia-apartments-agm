About the home

The kind of find people tell their friends about.

Cavernous by Seattle standards — a two-bedroom, two-bath layout that gives every roommate, partner, or family member their own space, without compromise.

“Upper-floor homes capture breathtaking views of the Magnolia neighborhood and the Seattle skyline beyond.”

Nine homes · 1 BR – 2 BR · Magnolia, Seattle

Magnolia Crestview is a small, well-kept building with a handful of homes — bright one-bedrooms and generously sized two-bedrooms. The two-bedroom plans are spacious by Seattle standards: full updated kitchens, two bathrooms, abundant closet space, and private decks with built-in storage.

Every layout was drawn to maximize daily livability. Real bedrooms with real closets. Pass-through kitchens, not galleys. Outdoor space that's actually yours. It's a building that works whether you're solo, partnered, or sharing the rent.

Homes don't come available often, and when they do, they go fast. Nine are open now.
